LAW I

The First Linguistic Annotation Workshop (LAW I) was held in Prague in conjunction with ACL 2007.

The LAW I merged NLPXML: Natural Language Processing and XML and FLAC: Frontiers in Linguistically Annotated Corpora. FLAC was a merger between Linguistically Interpreted Corpora (LINC) and Frontiers in Corpus Annotation (FCA). In total, the LAW I was the convergence of fourteen previous workshops (5 NLPXML, 1 FLAC, 6 LINC and 2 FCA).
