Joining SIGANN

Membership in SIGANN is free and open to all ACL members.

Joining SIGANN involves simply subscribing to the group list.

To subscribe to the list, send an email message with the word "subscribe" in the Subject: field to sigann-request@cs.vassar.edu.

To unsubscribe from the list, send a message with the word "unsubscribe" in the Subject: field to sigann-request@cs.vassar.edu

To change your email address on the list, first send an unsubscribe message for the old address to sigann-request@cs.vassar.edu. This can be done from the new address by putting word "unsubscribe" followed by the old email address in the message subject line (e.g., Subject: unsubscribe me@home.org). Once the old address is unsubscribed, send a subscribe message for the new address as shown above.
