Joining SIGANN

Membership in SIGANN is free and open to all ACL members.

Joining SIGANN involves simply subscribing to the group list.

To subscribe to the list, send an email message with the word "Subscribe" as the Subject: field to sigann-members+subscribe@googlegroups.com.

To unsubscribe from the list, send a message with the word "Unsubscribe" as the Subject: field to sigann-members+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com

To change your email address on the list, first send an unsubscribe message for the old address to sigann-members+unsubscribe@googlegroups.com. Once the old address is unsubscribed, send a subscribe message for the new address as shown above.
